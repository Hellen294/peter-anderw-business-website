💡 What Is a Grant?

A grant is a non-repayable sum of money awarded by a government, foundation, or organization for a specific purpose. Whether you're starting a business, expanding a nonprofit, or launching a creative project, grants can provide crucial funding.

  • 🆓 No repayment required
  • 📝 Requires an application or proposal
  • 🏆 Competitive selection process
  • 🔍 Often includes specific usage requirements
